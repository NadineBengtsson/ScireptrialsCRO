About Us

Scireptrials is a clinical research organization (CRO) founded in 2015 by Marcela Otero. Experienced in clinical trials worldwide, Marcela envisioned a company which would help relief the pain of those suffering chronical diseases such as cancer, diabetes and other illnesses and brought Scireptrials to life, a company based in Argentina, in order to unite life sciences and technologies and ultimately contribute to the patient's health. We are focused on helping patients, working in a sustainable and responsible way to generate value for society, offering a high quality services, with personalized solutions, at competitive prices.

Meet our founder and director

PhD. Marcela Otero

While working in a clinical research organization worldwide, she obtained a doctorate in biochemistry and an master of business administration. With her basis as biologist and more than 10 years of experience in clinical trails management, PhD. Marcela Otero has an unprecedented academic and professional background. She has also published academic papers and managed R&D projects. She has transformed a simple start-up to a recognized clinical research organization in a few years. Since 2015, she has been in charge as general manager and director of Scireptrails. In her role as director, Marcela manages R&D projects, training CRAs, and developing ongoing clinical trials.
