Our expertise

Scireptrials provides a comprehensive and integral clinical trials management service in all therapeutic fields. With experience in the fields of infectious diseases, dermatology, gastroenterology, rare diseases, in Phase II, Phase III, Phase IV, between others. In 2018, the company joined the ACROM alliance and is currently the local legal representative for clinical treatments of ILS Research laboratory in Argentina. Alongside ILS Research, Scireptrials most recent activities include two clinical studies conducted in Argentina. We have aided ILS in every regulatory and customs matters to be able to import the drug and file before the ANMAT (Argentinean regulatory authority).Scireptrails is also in the process of currently publishing final results of an observational clinical study which begun in 2017. With new projects in the horizon, we continue to grow in a sustainable manner.
