Our Solutions

Advising and collaborating with companies in every stage of the integrated drug development and clinical trial process

Clinical Trials Management

Equipped with a web-based and sophisticated electronic capture data system (EDC) used for database management, we offer an excellent electronic pharmacovigilance service. Through this system, quality of the data collected can be validated, it is trazable and easily auditable. It can safely gather, analyze and trace the effects of the drug under study and patients data in real time. In the event of detecting a risk, Scireptrials can easily mitigate said risk by sharing relevant information with regulatory authorities and other stakeholders to guarantee critical patients data and confidential information is protected. The system is compliant with data protection and transparency policies (21 CFR 11) and ICH. We also provide Risk-based monitoring (RACT) services on demand and produce and manage eCRFs with a top-notch versatile pharmacovigilance platform, managed by trained pharmacologists, doctors, system engineers, and expert biostatisticians.

Our services in every stage of your trail

Pre site selection and recruitment

Scireptrials could be contacted by sponsors, to select investigators for a trial, producing a list of sites which will be forwarded to the sponsor, and if confirmed, a CRA contacts the site and notifies the investigator was selected and calls him for a Pre Site Visit in which the site is evaluated by the sponsor that will select sites between them. We also help sites to recruit patients if needed and provide training of staff and site personnel.

Relevant documentation

Aiding sponsors in Medical Writing of clinical trial documents, by producing Protocols, Amendments, Informed Consent Forms, edition, monitoring reports, translation and regulatory reviewing. Submit Regulatory agencies periodic or final clinical trial Reports or pharmacovigilance reports, to the Regulatory agencies when needed. SCIREPTRIALS SRL also produces Clinical Study reports and trial data publications,

Submission to Regulatory Agencies

Acting as representative of the sponsor can submit the clinical trial to the local regulatory agency, and when the clinical trial is approved, SCIREPTRIALS SRL can be importer of the drug, with the help of the custom broker, manage the import of the clinical trial drug, necessary to start or continue the trial. Once the drug is distributed, the sites can Initiate to recruit patients for the trial. SCIREPTRIALS SRL, can also submit the required Periodic or Final Reports or pharmacovigilance reports, to the Regulatory agencies. Also, perform Quality assurance, of documents and Audit clinical trials.

Monitoring visits and reports

Perform through the selected CRA, routine site visits, including pre-study, initiation, interim, and closeout visits. It also includes responsibilities with minimal support from management. Visits to include monitoring of proper informed consent procedures, compliance with protocol, GCP/ICH Guidelines and other applicable regulatory requirements, and assurance of good site performance. This is accomplished by detailed review of subject records, essential documents, investigational product disposition and accountability, site personnel and procedures. Manage assigned sites by regular contacts to ensure site compliance, adequate enrollment, and understanding of study requirements. Submit to project manager, a completed Monitoring report after each visit for review

Closing up a clinical trial

Through the CRA, will check all regulatory documentation, all CRFs are complete and available to review, queries have to be complete or must be completed by the site, and all Medical records and source documents were reviewed. Also, must be sure that the drug accountability has been checked and is complete, and will do an list with all medication and returns for destruction. A final Report has to be completed and submitted to the legal entity. SCIREPRIALS SRL will support the closing of the clinical trial in all the steps, and will also make a final report to submit to regulatory agencies.
