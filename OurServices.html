Clinical Trials Management

Equipped with a web-based and sophisticated electronic capture data system (EDC) used for database management, we offer an excellent electronic pharmacovigilance service. Through this system, quality of the data recollected can be validated, it is trazable and easily auditable. It can safely gather, analyze and trace the effects of the drug under study and patients data in real time. In the event of detecting a risk, Scireptrials can easily mitigate said risk by sharing relevant information with regulatory authorities and other stakeholders to guarantee critical patients data and confidential information is protected. The system is compliant with data protection and transparency policies (21 CFR 11) and ICH. We also provide Risk-based monitoring (RACT) services on demand and produce and manage eCRFs with a top-notch versatile pharmacovigilance platform, managed by trained pharmacologists, doctors, system engineers, and expert biostatisticians.

Research and Development

Remarkable advances of technology and science and human-centered approaches to clinical development have resulted in improved drug development processes and heightened biotechnological innovations. In Scireptrials we strongly believe that these advances can significantly improve the general population's health standards and welfare. Scireptrials invests in investigating and testing potential solutions for the life sciences industry and to help companies shape the future of health. In our labs, Scireptrails counts with high-end technologies to ensure accuracy and to reach fruitful results. In this direction, we are equipped with a modern bioprinter, laminar flow, centrifuges, optic microscopes, DNA sequencing materials and heated insulators - to name a few - for research and experimentation. Following a clean and ordered scientific method, Dr. Marcela Otero is constantly working and researching on new bioprinting techniques to apply to her ongoing R&D projects.
