Clinical Trial Management

Equipped with a web-based and sophisticated electronic capture data system (EDC) used for database management, we offer an excellent electronic pharmacovigilance service. Through this system, quality of the data recollected can be validated, traced and it is easily auditable. It can safely gather, analyze and monitor the effects of the drug under study and patients data in real time. In the event of detecting a risk, Scireptrails can easily mitigate said risk by sharing relevant information with regulatory authorities and other stakeholders to guarantee critical patients data and confidential information is protected. The system is compliant with data protection and transparency policies (21 CFR 11) and ICH. We also provide risk-based monitoring (RACT) services on demand and produce and manage eCRFs with a top-notch versatile pharmacovigilance platform, managed by trained pharmacologists, doctors, system engineers, and expert biostatisticians.
